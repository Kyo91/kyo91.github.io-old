About Me

Mon Feb 12, 2018 by

Hi, my name is Sam Blumenthal. I’m a programmer with wide interests, especially functional programming and data technologies. I’m a recent Georgia Tech graduate working my first full-time job and am still getting used to working as a software engineer. In my free time, I enjoy not only hobby programming but also weightlifting, biking, and cooking.

As a TA in college, I learned material in greater depth and breadth than I ever did as a student. Similarly, I hope that you find this blog informative but I know that writing it will be for me. As a master of nothing but an apprentice of many, I have a lot to learn and a lot to write about.
